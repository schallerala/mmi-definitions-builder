\begin{dfn}
The \emph{Laplacian matrix} of a graph $G$ is
\[
L = D - A,
\]
where $D$ is the degree matrix, that is a diagonal matrix with $d_{ii} = \deg v_i$, and $A$ is the adjacency matrix of $G$.
\end{dfn}
