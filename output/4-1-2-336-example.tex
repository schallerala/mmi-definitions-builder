\begin{exl}
Rectification of
\[
\exists x \forall y P(x,y) \to \forall y \exists x P(x,y)
\]
produces, for example,
\[
\exists z \forall t P(z,t) \to \forall y \exists x P(x,y).
\]
\end{exl}
