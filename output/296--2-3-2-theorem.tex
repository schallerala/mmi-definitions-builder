\begin{thm}[Euler]
Let $G = (V, E)$ be a connected plane graph.
Denote by $F$ the set of its faces.
Then we have
\[
|V| - |E| + |F| = 2.
\]
In particular, the number of faces does not depend on the choice of an embedding.
\end{thm}
