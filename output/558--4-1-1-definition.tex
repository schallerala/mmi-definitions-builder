\begin{dfn}
A \emph{signature} is a triple of sets $(\cV, \cF, \cP)$ together with two maps $\cF \to \N \cup \{0\}$ and $\cP \to \N \cup \{0\}$
which describe the \emph{arity} of functions and predicates.
\end{dfn}
