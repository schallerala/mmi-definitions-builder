\begin{exl}
The set of all binary words whose tenth symbol from the right is $1$ can be described by a regular expression $(0+1)^* 1 (0+1)^9$,
where $(0+1)^9$ denotes $\underbrace{(0+1)\cdots (0+1)}_{9}$.
\end{exl}
