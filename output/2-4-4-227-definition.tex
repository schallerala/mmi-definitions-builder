\begin{dfn}
Let $G = (V, E)$ be a graph.
For any subset $S \subset V$ of the vertex set denote by $N(S)$ the set of all vertices adjacent to vertices in $S$.
The set $N(S)$ is called the \emph{neighbor set} of $S$.
\end{dfn}
