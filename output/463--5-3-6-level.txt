Ferrers diagrams
Ferrers diagram is a method to visualize a partition.
A partition

n = k_1 + + k_m, k_1 k_m

is represented by by  rows of dots, aligned on the left, with  dots in the first row,  dots in the second row etc.
Instead of dots one can use squares, see Figure fig:Ferrers.
