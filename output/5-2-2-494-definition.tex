\begin{dfn}
For $A(x) = \sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k x^k$ and $B(x) = \sum_{k=0}^\infty b_k x^k$ define the \emph{composition} as
\[
A(B(x)) = \sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k (B(x))^k.
\]
\end{dfn}
