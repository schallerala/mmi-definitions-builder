\begin{dfn}
A \emph{weighted graph} is a pair $(G, \omega)$, where $G$ is a usual graph, and
\[
\omega \colon E(G) \to \R
\]
is a map. The number $\omega(e)$ is called the \emph{weight} of the edge $e$.
\end{dfn}
