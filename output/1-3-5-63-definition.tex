\begin{dfn}
A \emph{composition} of a positive integer $n$ is a representation of $n$ as the sum of positive integers.
The order of summands plays the role, for example $5 = 3+1+1$ and $5=1+3+1$ are different compositions of $5$.
\end{dfn}
