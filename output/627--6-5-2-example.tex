\begin{exl}
Let us remove the unit productions from the grammar
\begin{align*}
S &\to A \mid B\\
A &\to AA \mid 0\\
B &\to BB \mid 1.
\end{align*}
The unit pairs are $(S,A)$ and $(S,B)$.
Remove the unit productions and introduce all $S \to \alpha$ for which $A \to \alpha$ or $B \to \alpha$:
\begin{align*}
S &\to AA \mid BB \mid 0 \mid 1\\
A &\to AA \mid 0\\
B &\to BB \mid 1.
\end{align*}
\end{exl}
