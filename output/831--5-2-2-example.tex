\begin{exl}
One has
\[
(1-x)^{-1} = 1 + x + x^2 + \cdots,
\]
as the brackets expansion
\[
(1-x)(1+x+x^2+\cdots) = 1 + (x - x) + (x^2 - x^2) + \cdots = 1
\]
shows.
\end{exl}
