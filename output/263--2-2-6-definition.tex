\begin{dfn}
A \emph{minimum spanning tree} of a weighted connected graph $(G, \omega)$ is a spanning tree $(V, E')$ of $G$ such that
the sum $\sum_{e \in E'} \omega(e)$ has the minimum possible value among all spanning trees of $G$.
\end{dfn}
