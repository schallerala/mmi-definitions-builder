\begin{dfn}
The formal power series
\[
a_0 + a_1 x + a_2 x^2 + \cdots = \sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k x^k
\]
is called the \emph{generating function} of the sequence $a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots$.
\end{dfn}
