
\begin{exc}
The graph shown in Figure \ref{fig:Petersen} is called \emph{Petersen graph}.
Show that it is isomorphic to the following graph:
\begin{gather*}
V = \{\text{all two-element subsets of }\{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}\},\\
E = \{\{A, B\} \mid A \cap B = \emptyset\}
\end{gather*}
\end{exc}
