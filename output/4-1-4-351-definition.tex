\begin{dfn}
One says that a structure $M$ \emph{satisfies} a closed formula $\phi$, if $\phi$ evaluates to true under $M$.
In this case $M$ is also called a \emph{model} of $\phi$, and one writes $M \vDash \phi$.
\end{dfn}
